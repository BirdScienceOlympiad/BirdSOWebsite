The BirdSO Mini Invitational for Divisions B and C will run from December 11–18, 2021. Featuring nineteen official events and five trial events, the tournament will take place in a virtual format where students will compete from the comfort of their own homes. The tournament will kick off with an opening ceremony and conclude with an awards ceremony where we will award medals for the top fifteen places and trophies for the top fifteen teams.

Format

The Mini tournament will be run in the mini SO format, described in the Rules Manual. Students must compete from their homes and may not meet up in-person. In addition, our tournament is asynchronous; teams can participate in any of the events at any given 50-minute interval during the duration of the competition (December 11–18, 2021). Teams from the same school are encouraged to take tests at the same time to ensure academic integrity.

Events

The event slate for the Satellite Tournament features twenty-two official events, five trial events, and five Computer Science Initiative events. Events will try to follow the official Rules Manual; however, some modifications may be necessary to adapt to our virtual format. Competitors are encouraged to review sections of the Event Modifications documents relevant to their events prior to the tournament.

Policies

All competitors are expected to follow Science Olympiad rules (both event-specific and General Rules), our Academic Honesty Policy, and the additional general policies included below.

  • All notesheets, for events that allow 8.5 by 11 inch sheet(s) of paper, must be printed out. For these events as well as events that do not allow any external resources, event supervisors will keep track of "time out of browser."
  • Mini Only: For events that allow a binder, online binders are permitted.
  • Mini Only: Participants may use any method of communication such as video call, voice chat, or the built-in Scilympiad chat. Partners may not gather in-person while participating in events.
  • All test events are 50 minutes long.
  • No-shows will result in [N + 1] points. Disqualifications as a result of cheating will result in [N + 2] points.
  • Events will follow national-level rules if applicable.
  • All video submissions and practice logs will be properly disposed of two weeks (14 days) after competition. No build videos will be saved.

Ceremonies

The Mini Tournament will feature a virtual Opening Ceremony on December 10, 2021, at 5:00 PM Pacific Time to kickoff our exciting week of competition. After the competition has concluded, we will host a virtual Awards Ceremony on December 23, 2021, at 5:00 PM Pacific Time where we will recognize the top finishers of both divisions in each event and overall. After the Awards Ceremony, medals and trophies will be mailed to coaches, and graded tests will be made available for study on Scilympiad.

Links to our ceremonies, which will be broadcast on our YouTube channel, will be shared here ahead of each. We hope you'll join us for these exciting events!
