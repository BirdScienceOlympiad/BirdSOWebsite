About

Bird Science Olympiad (also called BirdSO) was founded in December 2020 by a group of enthusiastic and experienced Science Olympiad competitors and alumni with the mission of providing Science Olympiad students with a high-quality competition experience during a mostly virtual season. During its inaugural season, BirdSO hosted an online Mini tournament for both Divisions B and C, which saw 62 and 121 teams, respectively, from across the United States. During its second season, BirdSO hosted two invitationals: a virtual Mini tournament in December 2021 and an in-person, at-your-school Satellite tournament in February 2022.

This season, BirdSO is hosting its familiar at-your-school Satellite tournament in February 2024, as well as one In-Person tournaments combining Division B and C at USC on January 13th, 2024.
