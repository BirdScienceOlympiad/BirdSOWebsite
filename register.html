Tournaments

Thank you for your interest in competing in the BirdSO Invitationals! This year, we will be hosting two Division B/C tournaments, a Mini Tournament and a Satellite Tournament. The Mini Tournament, which takes place from the comfort of your own home, will run from December 11–18, 2021. The Satellite Tournament, which takes place at your school under the supervision of your coach, will run February 12–19, 2022.

Mini Tournament: December 11–18, 2021
Satellite Tournament: February 12–19, 2022

Regardless of which tournament format you choose to participate in, we strive to deliver the same high quality competition experience for which BirdSO is known.

Register

Registration for the Mini and Satellite Tournaments opened on September 17, 2021, at 3:00 PM Pacific Time. Registration for the Mini Tournament will close on December 7, 2021 at 11:59 PM Pacific Time, and registration for the Satellite Tournament will close on February 8, 2022, at 11:59 PM Pacific Time. All teams registering after we have reached capacity will be placed on a waitlist for that tournament.

To register, please have your coach fill out the Google Form linked below that corresponds to the tournament(s) you are interested in. (If you are interested in both Mini and Satellite you must fill out both forms.) We will follow up with the coach whose contact information is provided to complete sign-up on the Scilympiad platform.

Payment of the registration fee is not required to secure your spot in a tournament but is required to compete. The instructions and deadlines for payments are listed below; we encourage you to pay at your earliest convenience to get logistics in order as early as possible.

Register for the Mini Tournament Register for the Satellite Tournament

Please note that per national Science Olympiad policy, you must be registered with your home state’s Science Olympiad organization to compete in Science Olympiad tournaments. Your team does not need to reside in Southern California to participate.

Payments

Registration fees are due three days before each tournament. For the Mini Tournament, the deadline is December 8, 2021, and for the Satellite Tournament, the deadline is February 9, 2022; both deadlines are at 11:59 PM Pacific Time.

You have two options for payment: you may either mail a check to the address below or you may use the embedded form on this page.

Southern California Science Olympiad
4321 W. 168th Street
Lawndale, California 90260

To pay using the embedded form, please select your tournament from the dropdown and enter the name of your school. After clicking "Pay Now" you will be redirected to a PayPal page where you may complete the payment process. If you are paying for both tournaments or multiple teams, you may need to complete this process several times.

All payments will be processed by Southern California Science Olympiad. You may check the registration and payment status for your team(s) here.
